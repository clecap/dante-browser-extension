Connect Extension to DanteWiki

The extension needs to identify the DanteWiki with which it will be used.

To establish this, please use a different browser window to navigate to the Main Page of your DanteWiki.

Copy the URL into the clipboard and paste it into the field below.

This window will then close automatically and you are all set!
